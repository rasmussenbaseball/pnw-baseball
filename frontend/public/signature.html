Install in Gmail

    
    
      
  1. Click Copy signature to clipboard above.
    2. 
      
  2. Open Gmail (signed in as your @nwbaseballstats.com account if you have one, otherwise your personal Gmail).
    3. 
      
  3. Click the gear icon ⚙ in the top right → See all settings.
    4. 
      
  4. Stay on the General tab. Scroll down to the Signature section.
    5. 
      
  5. Click + Create new. Name it something like NWBB. Click Create.
    6. 
      
  6. Click inside the empty signature editor box, then paste (Cmd+V on Mac, Ctrl+V on Windows).
    7. 
      
  7. Under Signature defaults (just below the editor), set both dropdowns to NWBB:
        
      
          
    • For new emails use: NWBB
      • 
          
    • On reply/forward use: NWBB
      • 
        
    
      
    8. 
      
  8. Optional: check "Insert signature before quoted text in replies".
    9. 
      
  9. Scroll to the bottom and click Save Changes.
    10. 
    

    

      Send yourself a test email after saving to make sure the logo renders and links work.
